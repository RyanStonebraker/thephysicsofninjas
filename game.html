Instructions:

The game works with a series of levels. At the top of the screen is the current objective of
the ninja and at the bottom is an explanation of the physics currently taking place. If the
current objective is completed, the ninja will move on to the next level. If the level is
failed, it will restart. Keys used to control the ninja will vary on each level and will be
displayed with the objective.
